About Me

I am a petroleum technologist and power engineer with experience in drilling and completion operations to petroleum distribution systems and power generation.

During the start of the pandemic, a chance encounter with an article led me to being infected with the programming bug. I have not looked back since, and decided to commit full-time towards learning web development. I fell in love with TypeScript and completed the Front-End portion of The Odin Project. I am currently working on the Back-End portion through Full Stack Open.

My background in engineering has taught me the importance of attention to detail, safety and creative problem solving. The projects and technologies I use reflect this philosophy. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly and accessible web applications that solve real-world problems.

I'm here because I love programming and the web, and am always looking to learn and grow. If you are interested discussing the value I bring for your team, please feel free to reach out!

Projects

Battleship

Battleship game set in the Honorverse and inspired by the movie War Games. The game features a tactical overview and real-time contextual battle messages. The setting of the game was inspired by my love of sci-fi novels and 80's sci-fi movies. The theme and colours are designed to mimic an Apple III monitor and the displays used in classic 80's movies.

Byblos

Byblos is a free web app that allows you to explore an endless world of literature, all stored in one convenient location. You can search and preview any book before making a commitment. Once finished, rate and favorite your most beloved reads and organize them in your personal bookshelf. Never lose track of your favorites again and easily access them whenever you need them.

Memoji Card

A simple app that allows you to create a custom Memoji card. The app uses the react-memoji npm package to generate the Memoji card. The app also allows you to save your Memoji card as a png image.

Calculator

A TDD remake of my Odin Project calculator project using React, TypeScript, Next.js, Tailwind, Styled-Components, Cypress, with assets from Frontend Mentor

Password Generator

A simple password generator app that generates a random string based on user selecteable parameters such as length, lowercase, uppercase, numeric, and/or special characters. The package used is the random npm library, which uses Math.random() as the underlying PRNG. The app also provides a strength rating for the generated password.

Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock

This is an implementation of Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock, a variant of the classic game Rock-Paper-Scissors. The game is played against the computer, who each choose from one of five available options.
